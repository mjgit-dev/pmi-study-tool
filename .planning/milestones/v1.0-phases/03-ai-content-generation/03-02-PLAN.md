Verification steps for the user:
1. Open 2-3 of the generated .md files in `processor/output/`
2. Read the `## Practice Questions` section in each file
3. Check each question against these criteria:
   - Is the scenario grounded in THIS lecture's specific content? (references a concept, term, or example from the transcript)
   - Is it a situational/scenario question, NOT a recall question? ("What should the PM do..." not "What is the definition of...")
   - Does the answer explanation cover why the correct answer is right AND why each wrong answer is wrong?
4. Read the `## Flashcards` section:
   - Are the terms PMP-relevant concepts from the lecture?
   - Are definitions 1-2 sentences and quiz-ready?
5. If questions look generic or not grounded in the lecture, say "needs revision" with specifics
6. If questions are well-grounded and scenario-based, say "approved"
